WHAT IS FOTON RACING?

Foton Racing is a group of 6 friends from high school in Grójec. We share same passion and desire to achieve great feats, what's more we believe in succeeding. After winning competition in Poland we qualify for grand final of "F1 In Schools" in Singapore. So far, our job was to build model of vehicle, present his technical features on track (powered by compressed CO2), build exposition and create portfolio of all these activities. In the meantime we have attended many extremely helpful meetings and trainings.

F1 IN SCHOOLS?

F1 In Schools is known to be biggest and most exciting educational project in the world, based on STEM (Sciences-Technology-Engineeering-Mathematics). It was founded back in 1999, and now it is held in over 50 countries. It is promoting philosophy of F1 among teenagers between 9 and 19 years old. “F1 In Schools” is non-profit organisation, so it is important for the participating teams to acquire sponsors and manage their budget wisely. This challenge inspires students to use IT to learn physics, aerodynamics, designing, production, branding, graphics, sponsoring, marketing, leadership / teamwork, media related skill and finance strategy.

WHAT'S NEXT?
