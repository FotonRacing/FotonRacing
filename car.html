WINNING VEHICLE

Foton Racing's bolide is constructed from ultralight material - polyurethane foam. Thanks to its low weight, its average speed is 70km/h, which means that it can travel across 20 meters tall track in only one second. Despite this, we still try our best to improve his average speed, since our bolide will compete with the best vehicles on world finals of “F1 in Schools” in Singapore
